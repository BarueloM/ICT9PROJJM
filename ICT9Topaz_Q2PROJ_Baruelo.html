The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia known for its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. With over 7,000 islands, the country boasts beautiful beaches, lush mountains, and diverse marine life.

These are my recommened places that you should visit!

Baguio

is a picturesque city nestled in the mountains of Luzon. Known for its cool climate, scenic parks, and vibrant flower festival, Baguio attracts visitors seeking a refreshing escape from the lowland heat.

Bohol

a province in the Philippines, is renowned for its unique natural wonders, most notably the famous Chocolate Hills, a series of 1,268 cone-shaped limestone hills that turn brown during the dry season, resembling giant chocolate kisses.

Cebu City

located in the central part of the Philippines, is a bustling metropolis known for its rich history, vibrant markets, and lively street food scene. As a major economic hub, it also serves as a gateway to the stunning beaches and diving spots of the surrounding Visayan Islands.
